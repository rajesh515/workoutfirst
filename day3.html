Day 3 - Upper Body Strength Training

Exercises:

  1. Bench press: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  2. Dumbbell rows: 3 sets of 12-15 reps (per arm)
  3. Pull-ups or lat pulldowns: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  4. Dumbbell flyes: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  5. Bicep curls: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  6. Tricep pushdowns: 3 sets of 12-15 reps

Instructions:

For each exercise, perform 12-15 repetitions per set. Rest for 1-2 minutes between sets. Choose a weight that challenges you, but allows you to complete all repetitions with good form.

Remember to warm up before starting your workout and cool down and stretch afterwards.
