Day 2 - Cardio and core

Workout:

  1. Warm-up: 5-10 minutes of light cardio (e.g. walking, cycling, or rowing)
  2. Treadmill intervals: 20 minutes (1 minute of sprinting followed by 1 minute of rest)
  3. Stationary bike: 20 minutes (alternating between 1 minute of high resistance and 1 minute of low resistance)
  4. Rowing machine: 10 minutes of steady-state rowing
  5. Plank: 3 sets of 30-60 seconds
  6. Russian twists: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  7. Bicycle crunches: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  8. Cool-down: 5-10 minutes of stretching
